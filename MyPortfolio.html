ABOUT

Chewie was born around May 2015 and got adopted in August by me from Tierheim Berlin, the largest animal home in Europe. He is very cute and funny. Chewie is an apartment cat who does not like to leave the apartment. Chewie is full of love, he is a wonderful pet. Through taking care of Chewie I learned about responsibilities, respects, and the meaning of life.
